Special Laws and Effects

  • 90-90 Rule: 90% of the work will take 90% of the time. The remaining 10% of the work will take 90% of the time
  • Abilene Paradox: The tendancy of a group to agree despite individuals holding differenct opinions
  • Akrasia Effect: Procrastination and Time Inconsistency
  • Apophenia: Seeing patterns in random data - can be a generalization of Confirmation Bias
  • Argumentum ad populum: If many believe it so, it is so
